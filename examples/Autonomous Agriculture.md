# Autonomous Agriculture (Drones and Unmanned Farms)

## Raw Data with Sources

### Market Scale
- Chinese agricultural drones completed work covering **173 million hectares in 2024**
- Market worth: ~13 billion yuan ($1.8 billion)
- Employment: nearly 500,000 people
- Market size: $2.1 billion (2023) → $2.4 billion (2024)
- Market consolidation: DJI holds 54.82% share, XAG holds 37.59%
- Source: [South China Morning Post](https://www.scmp.com/economy/china-economy/article/3292917/nowhere-go-chinas-farming-drones-take-root-industry-grows)

### Fully Unmanned Farms

#### Henan Province - First Fully Unmanned Wheat-Corn Farm
- Location: Qingfeng, Henan province
- Size: 3,500 mu (**233 hectares**)
- Designed by Henan Agricultural University ("sci-tech backyard program")
- Equipment: Driverless harvesters, tractors, air-suction seeders, precision irrigation, scouting drones
- Navigation: China's BeiDou satellite system
- Results:
  - Harvesting time: **7 days → 4 days**
  - Labor for water/fertilizer: **down 80%**
  - Overall human costs: **down 40%**
  - Efficiency: **up 30%**
- Source: [China Daily](https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202507/02/WS68649b6aa31000e9a5739b7c.html)

#### Shanghai Smart Farms Plan
- 12,000 mu across eight townships by end 2024
- Goal fulfilled by end 2025
- Equipment: Intelligent tractors, planting machines, smart sprayers, drones, combine harvesters
- Source: [South China Morning Post](https://www.scmp.com/economy/china-economy/article/3274342/shanghai-angles-acres-drone-grown-crops-automated-agriculture-plan)

### Drone Technology Advances

#### XAG P150 Drone
- Highest payload in international market: **70 kg**
- Flight speed: 18 m/s
- Source: [XAG News](https://www.xag.cn/en/news/official/xag/214)

#### Price-Performance Improvement
- **$2,100 per liter payload capacity (2014) → under $140 (2024)** - 90% reduction
- Modern features:
  - RTK positioning: ±2 cm accuracy
  - Obstacle avoidance via LiDAR
  - Variable-rate spraying: reduces chemical usage by 30-40%
- Source: [AgToday](https://agtoday.info/2025/07/05/china-agricultural-drone-industry-market/)

### Government Support
- 2025 No. 1 Central Document: First time low-altitude technology included alongside AI and big data as strategic farming tool
- Yunnan Province: Subsidies up to 14,400 yuan ($1,990) per drone
- Ningde, Fujian: Subsidized training fees up to 88,052 yuan (free training for farmers)
- January 2025: CAAC + Ministry of Agriculture released interim regulations on agricultural UAV operator training
- Source: [Global Times](https://www.globaltimes.cn/page/202503/1330357.shtml)

### Usage Statistics
- **98% of autonomous flight time** in China is agriculture-related (per XAG CEO Peng Bin)
- Source: [China Daily](https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202508/27/WS68ae58cea3108622abc9d536.html)
