# Xiaomi Case Study: Speed of Implementation

## Raw Data with Sources

### Timeline
| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| March 2021 | Announced intent to enter EV market |
| September 2021 | Xiaomi Automobile established, hired ~3,000 engineers |
| August 2023 | Received production permit from NDRC |
| December 2023 | Production started |

Source: [Wikipedia - Xiaomi SU7](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xiaomi_SU7)

### Speed Comparison
- Xiaomi SU7: **230 days** to 100,000 units
- Li Auto: ~1.9 years
- NIO: ~2.8 years
- XPeng: ~2.8 years
- Source: [Digitimes](https://www.digitimes.com/news/a20241114PD217/production-vehicle-xiaomi-xiaomi-su7-su7.html)

### Factory Operations

#### Phase 1 (Beijing Yizhuang)
- Partnership with BAIC for factory setup
- Capacity: **150,000 units/year**
- Production rate: ~40 cars/hour = **one every 76 seconds**
- Body Workshop: 100% automation on key processes
- Overall automation rate: **91%**
- 39 robots on assembly line
- Operates as "lights-out" facility
- Source: [emag Direct Industry](https://emag.directindustry.com/2024/07/29/inside-xiaomis-ev-factory-where-the-company-produces-an-electric-car-every-76-seconds/)

#### Expansion Speed
- Started factory extension on **July 26** - one day after buying the land
- Quote: "A speed that one cannot imagine in Europe, considering building laws"
- Source: [Electrive](https://www.electrive.com/2024/07/29/xiaomi-expands-production-capacity/)

### Supply Chain Strategy
- Leveraged existing Chinese "Factory City" infrastructure (originally built for Apple products)
- Outsourced components, kept production lean
- Cooperated with partners including Bosch (braking system, battery management)
- Result: **23.2% gross margin in Q1 2025**
- Source: [Bosch Global](https://www.bosch.com/stories/electromobility-in-china-xiaomi/)

### Sales Performance
- First 9 months: ~137,000 vehicles delivered
- Source: [Christopher Chico Substack](https://christopherchico.substack.com/p/xiaomi-builds-an-ev-every-76-seconds)

### Future Plans
- Beijing Phase II and Phase III factories in progress
- New Wuhan factory: production start May 2026
- Target: **1+ million EVs/year by 2026** (goal: 1.2 million)
- Source: [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2024/10/23/selling-ever-more-evs-xiaomi-ramps-up-factory-expansion/)

### Key Insight
Xiaomi demonstrates that the infrastructure for rapid, automated manufacturing already exists. A company with no automotive experience produced a competitive EV at scale in under 3 years by leveraging the ecosystem.
