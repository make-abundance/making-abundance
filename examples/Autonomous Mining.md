# Autonomous Mining

## Raw Data with Sources

### Fleet Deployments

#### China
- **Yimin Mine (Inner Mongolia)**: World's first fleet of **100 autonomous, all-electric mining trucks** (May 2025)
- Operated by China Huaneng Group
- Vehicles withstand extreme conditions: temperatures to **-48.5°C**
- Source: [Huawei](https://www.huawei.com/en/media-center/our-value/2025/driverless-trucks-yimin-mine)

#### Global Leaders
- **Caterpillar**: 690 autonomous trucks (end 2024), targeting **2,000+ by 2030**
- Source: [International Mining](https://im-mining.com/2025/11/07/caterpillar-sets-out-to-hit-over-2000-autonomous-mining-trucks-by-2030/)

- **BHP**: 367 autonomous trucks (July 2023) - largest fleet globally
- Source: [Mining Digital](https://miningdigital.com/digital-transformation/what-are-the-benefits-of-automation-in-mining-operations)

- **Suncor**: 140 autonomous trucks across Millennium and North Steepbank mines; 47+ at Fort Hills
- Source: [FutureBridge](https://www.futurebridge.com/industry/perspectives-industrial-manufacturing/autonomous-haulage-systems-the-future-of-mining-operations/)

### Full Autonomy Milestones

#### Volvo at Brønnøy Kalk (Norway)
- **2023**: Removed safety driver - industry first for active commercial operation
- **2024**: Started 24/7 operations
- Hauled **1+ million tonnes autonomously**
- Source: [Volvo Autonomous Solutions](https://www.volvoautonomoussolutions.com/en-en/autona-earth.html)

#### Resolute Mining (Mali)
- **Entire mining process autonomous**: drilling, charging, blasting, loading, trucking
- Results:
  - Gold production costs: **down $135/ounce**
  - Mining costs: **down 30%**
- Source: [Cyngn](https://www.cyngn.com/blog/the-rise-of-autonomous-mining-trucks-and-robots)

#### Caterpillar at Luck Stone Quarry
- "Go-Live" milestone: November 2024
- Fleet of autonomous Cat 777s reached **1 million tons hauled** by July 2025
- Also introduced first commercially available **Autonomous Water Truck** (Cat 789D AWT) - March 2025
- Source: [Caterpillar](https://www.caterpillar.com/en/news/caterpillarNews/2026/scaling-autonomy-system.html)

### Rio Tinto's "Mine of the Future"

#### AutoHaul (Autonomous Rail)
- World's first heavy-haul, long-distance autonomous rail operation
- **50 crewless trains** on 1,500km network
- Each train: 3 locomotives, transports **~28,000 tonnes** of iron ore
- Described as "the world's largest robot"
- Monitored remotely from Perth
- Source: [Rio Tinto](https://www.riotinto.com/en/about/innovation/automation)

#### Autonomous Drilling
- **World's largest autonomous drilling fleet**: 26 drills
- Operated remotely to safely and accurately drill blast-holes
- Source: [Mining Technology](https://www.mining-technology.com/features/mining-robots-rio-tinto-doubles-autonomous-drilling/)

#### Efficiency Gains
- Autonomous trucks: **15% more efficient** than manned vehicles
- Source: [Rio Tinto](https://www.riotinto.com/en/about/innovation/automation)

### Research and Development
- Rio Tinto partnership with University of Sydney (RTCMA): Over a decade, explicit goal of **fully autonomous mining operations**
- Research published in Communications Engineering: Demonstrated fully autonomous open-pit mine with heterogeneous machinery coordinated via parallel learning and digital twins
- Source: [Nature - Communications Engineering](https://www.nature.com/articles/s44172-024-00220-5)
